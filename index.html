My Favorite Hobby is
Doing Art

I love Art

My hobby is Art. Art makes me feel happy because it helps me express what I am feeling. It also helps to learn culture because it is visible in all cultures. It is fun to learn new techniques and use them to make our own art. There are many types of art, for example, we could use pens, paintbrushes, or pencils.
There are also artists who make up their own techniques, like for example, Jason Pollock splatter paint directly on the canvas, until he is satisfied.
There are many ways to do art, and you can also do it your own way. That is what I like about art, and why it is my favorite hobby.
